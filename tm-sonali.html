About me

Sonali, a Chemistry Graduate student with a passion for the unseen wonders of the world. Hailing from Punjab, India, she's always been fascinated by the hidden realms beyond the naked eye. Currently, she immerses herself in the Prasad Lab at the University at Buffalo, delving into the captivating field of nanomedicine. Her mission? To unravel the mysteries and develop innovative nano-treatments for neurodegenerative diseases. When Sonali isn't admiring her precious cells in the lab or exploring nature's beauty, she also takes an active role as a vice president of Graduate Chemistry Club (GCC) and as a member of the organizing committee for UB's GSS-2023 (Graduate Student Symposium), bringing her enthusiasm and organizational skills to contribute to this exciting event

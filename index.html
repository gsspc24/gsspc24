    Building Communication to Promote Green and Sustainable Practices

    Greetings, fellow chemists, collaborators, and curious minds! Welcome to the 19th edition of the GSSPC Symposium. If chemistry had its own tales of triumph, trials, and titrations, then the Graduate Student Symposium Planning Committee would undoubtedly be its chronicler—and what a tale we've spun since 2005!

    • Voice & Vision: Providing a national stage where the perspectives and paradigms of graduate students aren't just heard; they're celebrated.
    • Networking & Nurturing: Crafting spaces where graduates can not only meet seasoned professionals but also cultivate crucial leadership attributes.
    • Quality & Continuity: Being the wellspring from which the ACS Division of Chemical Education (CHED) draws consistently engaging and quality content.

    This symposium isn't just about chemistry; it's about chemists—especially those still amidst the thrilling challenges of graduate school.

About

Robert Dennis-Pelcher stands at the forefront of material science innovation, consistently driving transformative advancements in the industry. Serving as the Principal Scientist and Fillers Technology Council Chair at The Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company, Robert has been instrumental in steering pivotal projects and initiatives that have left an indelible mark on the sector.

Hailing from the State University of New York at Buffalo with a Ph.D. in Chemistry, Robert's academic prowess seamlessly dovetails with his real-world application. His ardor for sustainability, coupled with a sharp acumen in developing advanced materials, has carved him a niche as a forward-thinking materials scientist. This is not just about theory for Robert; his hands-on approach has played a significant role in the commercialization of end products developed from innovative raw materials. This holistic engagement is evident from his achievements; he has been pivotal in ushering in new source approval productivity savings to the tune of approximately $5 million over the past three years.

At the core of Robert's success is his unwavering team-first mentality, an attribute that allows him to seamlessly lead cross-functional teams. It's his ability to nurture collaboration while maintaining a laser focus on goals that has enabled him to consistently deliver game-changing results. A strong proponent of industry collaboration, Robert is also a member of the esteemed Royal Society of Chemistry.

For those seeking insights into the future of material science, especially in the realm of sustainability and advanced material development, Robert's vast experience, and pragmatic approach promise a treasure trove of knowledge and actionable strategies.

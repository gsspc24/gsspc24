About me

Udani is a first-year Ph.D. student working under the supervision of Dr. David Watson and Dr. Timothy Cook. She received her B.S. honors in Environmental Science from the University of Peradeniya Sri Lanka. After completing her B.S., she earned M.S. in Environmental Chemistry from SUNY College of Environmental Science and Forestry, Syracuse NY.

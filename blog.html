Are you ready to lead the next Graduate Student Symposium Planning Committee (GSSPC)? The GSSPC is instrumental in organizing and managing a symposium at the ACS national meeting, with complete autonomy over topic selection, fundraising, speaker recruitment, and logistical arrangements. Moreover, GSSPC groups play a crucial role in sustaining the program by recruiting and mentoring future GSSPC teams.

This is your chance to organize the Graduate Student Symposium at the Fall 2025 ACS National Meeting, taking place from August 18th-22nd in Colorado.

Application packet should contain the following:

  • Symposium logo (Can be changed later if your team is selected)
  • Symposium title
  • Description of your topic (Word limit: 150 words)
  • Why is your topic important and how is it related to the ACS Fall 2025 National Meeting theme? (Word limit: 200 words)
  • Goals of your symposium? – Point-wise preferred (Word limit: 100 words)
  • How will you achieve these goals? (Word limit: 150 words)
  • Proposed speakers – Names, photos, and short bios (Word limit: 150 words) (Can be changed later if your team is selected)
  • List of potential sponsors (Can be changed later if your team is selected)
  • Team members – Names, photos, and short bios (Word limit: 150 words each)

    • Visit this website for more details on the ACS GSSPC Program.

Dr. Silvia Luebben

About

Silvia Luebben is the visionary CEO and Founder of Sustainable Chemicals, LLC, with seventeen years of R&D expertise in polymer, chemistry, and material science. Her work is dedicated to developing sustainable, eco-friendly materials, steering her company to be at the vanguard of the sustainable materials field. Her team focuses on innovative recycling and repurposing methods, aiming for a circular economy and reduced environmental impact.

Luebben's R&D strategies revolve around sustainable solutions and the use of renewable resources in polymer development, leading to durable, biodegradable, and non-toxic materials. Employing advanced technologies like spectroscopy and computational modeling, she aims to predict and enhance material performance, marrying innovation with sustainability.

Through her leadership, Silvia Luebben is not just advancing material science but is also guiding the chemical industry towards a future of environmental harmony and innovation.

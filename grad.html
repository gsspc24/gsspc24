Travel Award Application

  1. Home
  2. Travel Award

The ACS Fall 2024 Graduate Student Symposium Planning Committee (GSSPC) is eager to facilitate student participation at the ACS Fall 2024 National Meeting through the provision of travel grants. Our symposium, titled "Breaking the Mold: Building Communication to Promote Green and Sustainable Practices," will spotlight cutting-edge approaches and strategies in promoting eco-friendly and sustainable methods, presented by a varied panel of esteemed speakers. All travel grant recipients are mandated to attend this full-day symposium during their presence at the national meeting.

Application submissions are now open and will conclude on June 15th, 2024 at 11:59 PM EST. Grants, in the form of reimbursements, will be accorded up to $500 to qualified graduate students from chemistry or related fields. The total number of grants provided will depend on the financial resources available to the GSSPC.

To initiate your application, please fill out the form provided below. Remember, the form must be completed in one go as it won't save if you exit midway. Successful applicants will receive notifications from the UB GSSPC by mid-May. For any clarifications or inquiries regarding the application process, you are welcome to contact us directly at gsspc@buffalo.edu.

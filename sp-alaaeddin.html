Dr. Alaaeddin Alsbaiee

About

Alaaeddin Alsbaiee, PhD, is the R&D Manager at BASF, where he blends his expertise in polymer chemistry with a deep commitment to sustainability. Holding a PhD in Polymer Chemistry and currently pursuing an MSc in Sustainability at Harvard, Dr. Alsbaiee embodies the bridge between cutting-edge chemical innovation and environmental stewardship. His leadership at BASF is marked by pioneering development in sustainable materials, focusing on the creation and application of advanced polymers that are both high-performing and environmentally friendly.

Dr. Alsbaiee’s work is at the forefront of the industry’s push towards sustainability, driving research that aims to reduce environmental impact without compromising material quality or functionality. Through his initiatives, he fosters collaboration across disciplines, leveraging his academic background to integrate sustainable practices into every phase of product development. His contributions not only advance BASF’s position as a leader in sustainable materials science but also underscore his personal dedication to creating solutions that benefit both society and the environment.
